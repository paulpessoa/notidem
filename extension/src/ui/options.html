How your keys are protected
  • Encrypted at rest on this device (AES-GCM) before being saved.
  • Stored only in this browser's local storage — never synced to the cloud.
  • Sent straight to the AI provider you choose; they never pass through our servers.
  • Never written to logs or the console.

No browser extension can fully protect a key against malware already running on your computer — to use a key, it must be decrypted in the browser. For maximum safety, use a scoped key with a low spend limit, and remove it when you're done.
